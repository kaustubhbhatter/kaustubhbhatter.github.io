Firstly, thanks for visiting my online resume. It may not be very glossy or flashy, but I can at least say that it is something that I built on my own. I was constantly wondering how do I make my resume look better. After all, the famous theory goes that recruiters rarely look at your resume for more than six seconds. So how do I make mine stand out? How do I make mine different? Doing so is not easy as it sounds as you mostly end up presenting yourself in a pretentious manner. It was then that I thought, why not bring my resume to the internet! So this is when I thought of putting my resume as a complete web-page. The idea worked out pretty well and thanks to this endeavour, I ended up learning HTML and CSS!
